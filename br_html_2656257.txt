Menard finished second in the Zippo 200 Xfinity Series race at Watkins Glen International Saturday afternoon.Menard piloted the No. 2 RCR Chevrolet this weekend at Watkins Glen International. It was a great performance for the veteran driver. He would race in the Top 5 and Top 10 all afternoon in this 82-lap event. When Brad Keselowski dropped out of second in the closing laps, Menard would make his best effort to run down Joey Logano but with no success. Menard crossed the finish line second for his fourth Top-5 finish of the season in just six starts.
