Edwards won the pole for the Cheez-It 355k at Watkins Glen International Saturday afternoon. He turned a fast lap of 126.562 mph.The Joe Gibbs Racing star built on the momentum of his Top-10 finish at Pocono last week with a fast lap at the New York road course. Edwards edged Kyle Larson in knockout qualifying to claim the top starting spot for the Cheez-It 355k. The veteran driver should crack the Top 5 in Sunday's race at the Glen.
